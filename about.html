About us

You'd think the person who came up with this idea is a plant master, but that is far from the truth

I'm not master and I have no green thumbs

Since I was old enough to understand what a plant was, I've always found them so interesting. However, I'm probably one of the worst plant parents out there. I wanted to be able to take care of plants without struggling with finding simple information that would help me do so. This is why I created Fiore. A simple, accessible, and useful tool to aid folks like me who don't have green thumbs but love green things.

Everyone deservers accessible knowledge

Information on the internet can be extremely word heavy. It's too much information, it's never sectioned in a more readable manner, and honestly, it just hurts my eyes. I wanted to make a website that would eliminate all of the issues of accessibility. Using fewer words to get the same amount of information and using plenty of spacing to make things easier to read. Also pictures. Who doesn't like a good picture book?
